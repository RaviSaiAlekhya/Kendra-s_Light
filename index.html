Our Story

Just two sisters, one big one small, two different realms but still connected. Physical & spiritual.

My sister Kendra passed away September 10, 2020, and since that day I've never been the same. I guess we all haven't been the same. She was the glue leaving behind 3 beautiful daughters & 2 handsome sons, a brother & a mother.

To know Kendra is to love Kendra. She was bold, loud & real. She was unapologetically her. Most importantly she loved no matter what was thrown her way.

My sister was my best friend, my right hand. We did everything together. The strength she carried and all she endured was admirable.

Turning pain into positivity because of her, she's still my inspiration and this is just a continuation of dedication to her.

With love, Ashley

Forever in our hearts - September 10, 2020
